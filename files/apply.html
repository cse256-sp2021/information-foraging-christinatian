Tuition


Tuition is paid by the year.

Parents who enroll their children for the first time will have to pay a deposit of one month of tuition ($500/month for half day students)($1105/month for full day students).

Application Procedure

Parents interested in Washington University Nursery School should contact the school to schedule a visit.

An application fee of $40 is required when applying to the nursery school. After this is received, your child is placed on an enrollment list. You will be notified of acceptance in the March preceding the fall enrollment date. Upon acceptance, a nonrefundable deposit of one month’s tuition is due. This will be applied to tuition for the following May.

You may fill out an Application for Admission online. You may also print out and return the pdf version: Application for Admission WUNS
